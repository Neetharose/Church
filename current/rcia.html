Called to be a Priest

Sometimes young or older men may have thoughts of being a priest, they may sense something moving them to be interested in the vocation even while in their early teens or later in life. Beginning as a call from God but often needing guidance, encouragement and discernment, the parish priest and assistant priests of your parish are always available to help you in this journey.

Called to be a Religious

The call to "religious service" also is a very important ministry in the Church where the Holy Spirit will inspire a person to a particular charism of service in the building up of God's kingdom on earth.

RCIA Sponsoring

An integral part of our life as baptised Christians is the importance of continuing our pilgrim journey of learning and growing in Christ through the ministry of his Church. Opportunities of increasing our faith through better learning and understanding of our faith and the Church's mission in Christ come in a number of ways. Any enquiry is most welcome and special evenings where topics may be discussed with priests or qualified persons can be easily arranged.

Other activities in the parish are a great means for growing in the faith such as being a sponsor for those participating in the RCIA. Parishioners are greatly encouraged to walk the journey of RCIA in the role as sponsor to someone seeking to become a member of the Catholic Church. The benefits in terms of growing in one's own faith are significant.
