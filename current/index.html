Office Hours

Parish Office Hours are from 9am to 12.30pm Monday to Friday.

Queries, information and arranging of appointments can be made during these times.

Information is readily available for most enquires by visiting by navigating through this website or visiting our parish Facebook page.

Outside these hours, if the matter is urgent especially for the Sacrament of Anointing of the Sick and or the Last Rites, please contact the parish and if the phone is unattended please leave a message.

The Priests plan appointments and parish engagements for the afternoons along with prayer time, homily preparation and rest. Please respect their time set aside these areas, but by all means if you need to see a priest urgently please ring the bell and or call.

  • St John Vianney Catholic Church: Salmon Gum Road, Kambalda
  • St Joseph Catholic Church: Prinsep Street, Norseman
  • St Mary Catholic Church: Lindsay Street, Coolgardie
  • Our Lady of Montserrat Catholic Church: 48 Altair Street, Southern Cross
